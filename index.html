Flowers of the Rainbow

This is a page about flowers organized by color and their meanings.

Red Flowers

Red flowers symbolize love and passion.

Popular Red Flowers

  • Roses
  • Tulips
  • Poppies

What They Represent

  1. Love
  2. Passion
  3. Energy

Orange Flowers

Orange flowers symbolize energy and enthusiasm.

Popular Orange Flowers

  • Marigolds
  • Daylilies
  • California Poppies

What They Represent

  1. Warmth
  2. Creativity
  3. Excitement

Yellow Flowers

Yellow flowers symbolize happiness and positivity.

Popular Yellow Flowers

  • Sunflowers
  • Daffodils
  • Daisies

What They Represent

  1. Happiness
  2. Friendship
  3. Optimism
