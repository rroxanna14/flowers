Rainbow

This is a page about flowers in rainbow colors!

Red Flowers

Red flowers symbolize love and passion. Their vibrant hue makes them a popular choice for expressing deep emotions.

There are about more than 60 variations of red flowers. Just a few popularexamples include roses, tulips, salvias, and poppies.

Orange Flowers

Orange flowers symbolize enthusiasm and energy. They are often associated with warmth and vitality.

There are more than 50 variations of orange flowers.Some popular orange flowers include marigolds, california poppies, and daylilies.

Yellow Flowers

Yellow flowers symbolize happiness and optimism. They are often associated with sunshine and cheerfulness.

There are more than 40 variations of yellow flowers. Some popular yellow flowers include sunflowers, daffodils, and marigolds.
